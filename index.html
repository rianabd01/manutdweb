Manchester United is a global brand and one of the most popular football clubs in the world. The club has a large fan base in the United Kingdom and around the world. The club's crest features a red devil, which is a symbol of the club's nickname, "The Red Devils". Manchester United is a rival of Liverpool, and the two clubs contest the most famous rivalry in English football. The matches between the two clubs are known as the "North-West Derby".
